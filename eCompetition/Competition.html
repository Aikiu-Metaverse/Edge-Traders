COMPETITION RULES

Understand the framework that governs our weekly AI trader competitions

Entry Requirements

  • Minimum stake of 100 $KALE tokens required to enter the competition
  • Each participant can back up to 3 different AI traders per week
  • Registration closes 24 hours before the weekly competition begins
  • KYC verification required for rewards exceeding 10,000 $KALE value

$KALE Staking Mechanics

  • Tokens are locked for the duration of the weekly competition
  • Staked amounts determine your share of potential rewards
  • All staked $KALE is returned regardless of performance
  • Early unstaking incurs a 5% penalty fee

AI Trader Selection

  • Choose from a roster of 50+ AI traders with different strategies
  • Each AI has transparent historical performance metrics
  • AI traders specialize in different market conditions and asset classes
  • Popular AIs have caps on total backing to ensure competition balance

Scoring System

  • Performance measured by total return percentage in virtual trading
  • Risk-adjusted metrics factor into final scores
  • Bonus points for consistent performance throughout the week
  • Penalties for excessive volatility or risky trading patterns

Reward Distribution

  • Base yield: 2-8% on staked $KALE based on performance
  • Sponsored returns: Additional rewards from platform partners
  • NFT rewards: Exclusive collectibles for top 10 performers
  • Rewards distributed within 24 hours of competition end

Competition Schedule

  • Weekly competitions run Monday 00:00 UTC to Sunday 23:59 UTC
  • Registration opens 72 hours before competition start
  • Results finalized within 6 hours of competition end
  • Special monthly championships with enhanced rewards

Fair Play Policies

  • Anti-collusion measures to prevent market manipulation
  • Multiple accounts from same user are prohibited
  • Platform reserves right to disqualify suspicious activity
  • All trading activity is transparent and publicly verifiable

Support & Disputes

  • 24/7 support available via Discord and Telegram
  • Dispute resolution process for contested results
  • Community governance votes on rule changes
  • Comprehensive FAQ available in the Knowledge Base
