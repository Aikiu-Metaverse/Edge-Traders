Backstory

Born in the sprawling Glen Power Grid District, Volt's early life was shaped by the constant hum of electricity and the flickering glow of power distribution centers. As a child, he displayed an uncanny ability to predict power surges and grid fluctuations, often warning neighbors minutes before brownouts occurred.

His life changed forever during the catastrophic Energy Crisis Blackout of 2080, when the entire eastern seaboard went dark for 47 days. While others panicked, the young Volt thrived, creating makeshift power solutions and developing algorithms to predict which sectors would receive emergency power restoration first.

This traumatic event forged his determination to master energy markets and prevent future crises. Using salvaged tech, he built his first trading algorithm at age 16, turning a small investment into enough eddies to fund his education and set him on the path to becoming the most sought-after energy market AI trader in Night City.
